How To Roleplay

Roleplaying is a unique experience many people enjoy around the world. People can roleplay in real life or they can enjoy it virtually. Either way roleplay is a great way to relax and preted to be whatever you want. The official definition of roleplay given by Google Dictionary is "act out or perform the part of a person or character, for example as a technique in training or psychotherapy." Roleplaying is more than that. It is a way people can get into character as anyone or anything they want to be. It is an escape from reality for some people. People may even gather up in real life to LARP,live-action roleplay.

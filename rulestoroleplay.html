Rules To Roleplay

When you begin your roleplay adventure you must make sure you follow the rules to maximize immersion for you and your partners. The rules are what make the roleplay community as great as it is. Follow the simple rules listed below to have the best roleplay experience.

1. Don't swap characters mid-roleplay.
2. Don't merge storylines without your partner's consent.
3. Don't kill another player's character without their consent.
4. Don't make yourself invincible.
5. Keep swearing to a minimum.
6. Refrain from roleplaying explicit content.
7. Obey the administrators.
8. Don't control another player's character.
9. No Harassment.
10. Have Fun!
